7 /*
8  * Written by Paul Riseborough 2013 to provide:
9  * - Combined control of speed and height using throttle to control
10  * total energy and pitch angle to control exchange of energy between
11  * potential and kinetic.
12  * Selectable speed or height priority modes when calculating pitch angle
13  * - Fallback mode when no airspeed measurement is available that
14  * sets throttle based on height rate demand and switches pitch angle control to
15  * height priority
16  * - Underspeed protection that demands maximum throttle switches pitch angle control
17  * to speed priority mode
18  * - Relative ease of tuning through use of intuitive time constant, trim rate and damping parameters and the use
19  * of easy to measure aircraft performance data
20  */
